Projects

CoVid-19 Mobility

In March 2020 I supported my former team at HU Berlin / RKI, ROCS, by co-analysing the reduction of trips from German citizens following the measures to slow the pandemic spread of CoVid-19. Our analysis showed about a 40% reduction of trip numbers overall, with a stronger reduction of trips between counties than within counties. Ongoing.

Mission Prescription

In December 2019 I attended the hackathon #HackingAMR organised by JPIAMR in Stockholm. The goal of the event was to develop and prototype data-driven products to further the fight against antimicrobial resistance. Team Mission Prescription focused on increasing the quality of antibiotic prescriptions in primary care by utilising local community-based antimicrobial resistance data to recommend the most appropriate antibiotic to prescribing physicians. We won 1st place at the event, and are currently evaluating how best to push our idea forward with the help of experienced mentors from JPIAMR. Ongoing.
